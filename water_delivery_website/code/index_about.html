How do we work?


Make the purchase through our website or app, providing information such as address and payment method, then we will come to your home within 40 minutes. We have free shipping for purchases of 40 liters or more.


How we started


Quero Água distributes mineral water and sells related products and accessories. It was founded in January 2023. The founding partners had experience with the public, sales, service with suppliers and administration, researched the point and the business, identified suppliers and customers and hired their employees. Thus they inaugurated Pivoto tele agua.

The name Pivoto tele agua was chosen because it is a family company, thus always caring for the safety of other families as well. We have two couriers who ride around on bicycles, plus a truck loaded with gallons for large purchases. It differs from competitors because it is a company formalized with legal bodies, especially because it has authorization to sell mineral water issued by the health department.

It aims to become known for the quality of service and standardization of its employees, to win new customers and satisfy existing ones, for this we work with news and promotions.
